Roasted New Red Potatoes

Roasted red potatoes at their best — plain and simple. Red potatoes are tossed with olive oil, salt, and pepper, then roasted to perfection.

Ingredietns

  • 2 pounds new red potatoes, halved
  • 1/4 cup olive oil
  • 1 teaspoon salt
  • 1/2 teaspoon black pepper
  • 1 tablespoon fresh rosemary, chopped (optional)
  • 1 tablespoon fresh thyme, chopped (optional)
  • 1 tablespoon garlic powder (optional)
  • 1 tablespoon onion powder (optional)
  • 1 tablespoon paprika (optional)
  • 1 tablespoon grated Parmesan cheese (optional)
  • 1 tablespoon fresh parsley, chopped (optional)
  • 1 tablespoon lemon juice (optional)
  • 1 tablespoon balsamic vinegar (optional)
  • 1 tablespoon Dijon mustard (optional)
  • 1 tablespoon honey (optional)
  • 1 tablespoon soy sauce (optional)
  • 1 tablespoon Worcestershire sauce (optional)
  • 1 tablespoon hot sauce (optional)
  • 1 tablespoon ketchup (optional)
  • 1 tablespoon mustard (optional)

instructions

  1. Preheat the oven to 425°F (220°C).
  2. In a large bowl, toss the halved potatoes with olive oil, salt, and pepper until evenly coated.
  3. If using, add any optional ingredients like rosemary, thyme, garlic powder, onion powder, paprika, or cheese and mix well.
  4. Spread the potatoes in a single layer on a baking sheet lined with parchment paper.
  5. Roast in the preheated oven for 25-30 minutes or until golden brown and crispy, flipping halfway through.
  6. Remove from the oven and let cool slightly before serving.
  7. If desired, drizzle with lemon juice or balsamic vinegar before serving.
  8. Garnish with fresh parsley if desired.
  9. Enjoy your delicious roasted new red potatoes!
